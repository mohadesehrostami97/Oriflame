Because we are from Sweden we have a different – and holistic – view on beauty. It’s not only about how you look, but how you live, feel and act. To us beauty is a way of life: to be healthy, enjoy beautiful skin, express yourself and have fun. That’s beauty by Sweden – our way of helping you live a healthier, more beautiful life.
